🎭 How to Play Mafia

← Back to Home

🎯 Objective

👥 Village Team

Eliminate all Mafia members through voting during the day phase.

🔪 Mafia Team

Eliminate villagers until you equal or outnumber them.

🔄 Game Flow

🌙 Night Phase

Mafia members secretly choose a villager to eliminate. Other special roles may also act during this phase.

☀️ Day Phase

The results of the night are announced. Players have time for silent contemplation and observation.

🗳️ Voting Phase

All players vote to eliminate someone they suspect is Mafia, or vote for "No Murder" to skip elimination.

👤 Roles (Phase 1)

👨‍🌾 Villager

Team: Village

Goal: Eliminate all Mafia through voting

Abilities: Vote during day phase

🔪 Mafia

Team: Mafia

Goal: Equal or outnumber the Village

Abilities: Kill one villager each night, vote during day phase

🏆 Win Conditions

  • Village Wins: All Mafia members are eliminated
  • Mafia Wins: Mafia equals or outnumbers the remaining villagers

📋 Game Rules

  • This is a silent game - no chat or voice communication
  • Use the "👁️ Reveal My Role" button to check your role at any time
  • Pay attention to voting patterns and night outcomes for clues
  • You can always vote for "No Murder" if you're unsure
  • When eliminated, your role is revealed to all players
  • Dead players cannot vote or take actions

💡 Strategy Tips

  • For Villagers: Watch for suspicious voting patterns and try to identify the Mafia
  • For Mafia: Blend in with the villagers and avoid drawing suspicion
  • General: The game is about deduction - use all available information wisely
